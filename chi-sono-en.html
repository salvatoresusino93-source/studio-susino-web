Dr. Salvatore Susino

Medical Doctor — Specialist in Radiology

About me

I am Salvatore Susino, a radiologist. I graduated in Medicine at the University of Catania in 2019 and specialised in Radiology at the Università del Piemonte Orientale in 2025.

Today I work in my own practice in Pozzallo, where I perform ultrasound scans by appointment. If anything in the report is unclear, I will explain it to you.

Registered with the Medical Association of Ragusa (no. 3071).

What I do at the practice

Most of my work is clinical ultrasound — abdomen, thyroid, musculoskeletal, Doppler and more. If your doctor has requested a scan and you can't find it on the site, just call us: often it is simply a different name for the same thing.

The practice

It is in the centre of Pozzallo, on Via dell'Arno 34. I see patients by appointment. You can book online or call +39 0932 954441 or +39 351 374 6102.
