Carotid Doppler ultrasound in Pozzallo

The exam that checks the blood flow in the carotid arteries, which carry blood to the brain. A simple, painless preventive step, without radiation. By appointment in Pozzallo (RG), Italy, with a report at the end.

What it is and what it is for

The Doppler ultrasound of the supra-aortic trunks studies the carotid arteries and the neck vessels that carry blood to the head. It assesses the blood flow and any presence of narrowing or plaques. It is one of the most useful cardiovascular prevention exams.

When to have it

It is particularly recommended with increasing age and in the presence of high blood pressure, high cholesterol, diabetes, smoking or a family history of cardiovascular problems. It is often requested by your doctor as a preventive check. Quite simply, it is an exam that brings peace of mind.

How it works and preparation

No preparation is needed. You lie down and I place the probe on your neck with a little gel, studying the flow in the carotid arteries. No needles, no radiation, no discomfort. The exam is complete in a few minutes and I give you the report, explaining what was found.

Frequently asked questions

What is it for?

To study the blood flow in the carotid arteries and detect narrowing or plaques. It is cardiovascular prevention.

Does it hurt? Is preparation needed?

No to both: painless, with no needles or radiation, and it requires no preparation.

Are supra-aortic trunks and carotids the same thing?

Yes: supra-aortic trunk Doppler and carotid Doppler refer to the same exam.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

For a private exam it is not required. If you have one, bring it along with any previous scans.
